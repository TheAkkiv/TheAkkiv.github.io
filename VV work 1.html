Lahes Elos?! Event

Lahes Elos?! Promotional Material

I created a poster and promotional social-media materials for "Lahes Elos?!" youth event organized by Lahti youth services as part of Masto Festarit which is a series of events organized by the city of Lahti.

The client wanted something with a urban / graffiti feel and optionally some imagery relating to the activities at the event. I made multiple different concepts for the client. This pink version is the one chosen by the client. There are a few other concept I sent at the bottom.
