Lahti Nuorusopalvelut Flyers

I created flyers for some of the different branches of Lahti youth-employment and trainning services, to be handed out at events. The flyers were made in the official Lahti city visual style, but each branch decided what they wanted on the cover and what the content of the flyer was.
